</b></p><p><span class="p-blue">alecks：</span>LOUD是去年Champions比赛的冠军，是一支高水平的队伍。能够与这样的对手交手是一件大事，所以我们感到有压力，但我们应该更好地保持冷静。我认为能够获胜是很好的。</p><p><b>――然而，在第二张地图：Split中，您输了2-13，差距很大。您认为有什么原因吗？</b></p><p><span class="p-blue">alecks：</span>原因很明显，首先是<b>选手之间的沟通</b>。平时我们会相互讨论应该做什么，不应该做什么，然后进行布局。但这次比赛，每个人都只是说“注意这个”、“注意那个”，没有人能够进行有效的指挥。结果，选手们开始躲在彼此的背后。像LOUD这样的高水平对手，不会放过这样的弱点，他们会彻底攻击，所以我们只剩下了失败。
</p><p><b>――对于LOUD，你们采取了什么样的对策？</b></p><p><span class="p-blue">alecks：</span>前一天和今天早上，我们重新观看了LOUD的所有比赛，研究了他们的模式。然后，<b>我几乎可以确定他们会选择莲花</b>。此外，我们预测他们也准备了珍珠，所以我们选择了分裂。然而，结果是我们在分裂中惨败了，所以下次我想选择珍珠（笑）。</p><p>在观看LOUD的比赛后，比赛开始前一个小时，我们和选手们讨论了游戏计划。我们有很多保留的战术，这次我们采用了其中的一些。准备得如此充分，接下来只需要在比赛中试一试了。</p><figure class="ctms-editor-image">![image](https://www.inside-games.jp/article/imgs/zoom/1226947.png)<figcaption>解说员岸大河也指出“PRX有优势”</figcaption></figure><h2>◆PRX的进攻秘诀是什么？</h2><p><b>――实际上，在这次采访之前，我们曾询问LOUD的选手Saadhak，他表示“PRX展示了新的游戏风格，所以战斗很有趣”。作为教练，你认为PRX的强处在哪里？</b></p>
</b></p><p><span class="p-blue">alecks：</span>我和整个团队的意见是，<b>在等待某些事情发生时耐心不够的玩家在《VALORANT》中太多了</b>。我们更喜欢快速的游戏方式，例如，当敌人使用了幽灵步技能后，我们会在45秒内采取行动。我们不使用大型游戏计划。我们准备了许多小型游戏计划，制定一个计划并在执行该计划的同时决定下一个计划。</p><p><b>——这种态度是如何在训练中培养的呢？</b></p><p><span class="p-blue">alecks：</span>在训练中，我们尽可能只练习一个计划，以便产生新的想法。Jason（f0rsakeN）经常提出想法，但最近所有选手都可以提出想法。我认为这是我们不断努力让他们<b>变得有创造力的结果</b>。</p><p>然后，一旦有了想法，就需要反复测试并判断其实用性。 </p>
We put to use what seems useful in practice and temporarily "save" what is unnecessary. The ideas saved here may be combined when new agents are added to the game. This way, we can adapt to new environments faster than other teams. In other words, our strength lies in our creativity. 

PRX moves too fast for even excellent switchers to keep up with, so how do the players communicate with each other? 

Alecks: PRX's belief is to trust that the player in front of them will aggressively attack, which is why we are aggressive. If we know that our teammates will move forward, we don't need much communication. We just put into practice what we've practiced. Our training may seem like we're fooling around, but we're actually learning a lot. 

![image](https://www.inside-games.jp/article/img/2021/05/17/1226948/1007720.html)
</p><p>如果第一个出击的选手无法与其他选手配合，那么追赶就会变得困难。因此，我们需要相互信任，配合第一个出击的选手。正如我之前所说，我们会同时执行一个计划，然后将另一个计划与之结合。基本上，如果第一个出击的选手呼叫了计划，其他选手可以立即判断应该如何配合。因为我们已经进行了充分的练习。总之，大量的练习非常重要。</p><h2>◆f0rsakeN是关键球员，但KAY/O不行</h2><p><b>——在Lotus战队中，您采用了罕见的组合，选择了Omen（mindfreak）和Astra（f0rsakeN）。这是怎么发生的呢？</b></p><p><span class="p-blue">alecks：</span>之前f0rsakeN使用Cypher时，他抱怨说无论是攻击还是防御都没有太大的用处。因为Cypher需要检查摄像头，所以他感觉部署速度太慢了。
Therefore, we decided to adopt Astra. </p><p><b>- What are the strengths of Astra?</b></p><p><span class="p-blue">Alecks:</span> With Astra, we can quickly interfere with enemy invasions. We came up with ideas like "this can be interesting, let's make it more interesting." We can throw smoke together and suppress or support like Viper.</p><p><b>- Is f0rsakeN the key to realizing various compositions?</b></p><p><span class="p-blue">Alecks:</span> It's <b>f0rsakeN</b>. He can come up with the most ideas, perform any role at a high level, and realize everything we want to do. But KAY/O is the only one that doesn't work (laughs). I don't know why, but KAY/O is terrible. </p>

如果不考虑这一点，f0rsakeN可以说是最重要的球员了。 </p><figure class="ctms-editor-image"><img src="https://www.inside-games.jp/imgs/zoom/1226949.png" class="inline-article-image" width="640" height="426"></figure><h2>◆对于Smith的评价是“满分10分中7分”</h2><p><b>——与Masters Tokyo不同，这次比赛中加入了something（Smith）加入了团队。您如何重新评价他？</b></p><p><span class="p-blue">alecks：</span>我会给他7分。他的表现已经足够好了，但<b>他还可以做得更好</b>。特别是比赛开始后，他有很大的改进空间，每场比赛都需要很长时间才能点燃引擎。他可能变得紧张了，或者游戏计划不合适。 </p><figure class="ctms-editor-image"><img src="https://www.inside-games.jp/imgs/zoom/1226950.png" class="inline-article-image" width="640" height="426"></figure><p><b>——在不熟悉的地方战斗可能会很辛苦。</b></p><p><span class="p-blue">alecks：</span>在国际比赛中，需要确立完善的身体和精神健康日常例行程序，包括睡眠和身体状况管理等。关于这一点，我们曾经雇用专门的体育心理学家来协助我们。
However, at that time, something was not yet on the team. Currently, I am also serving as a substitute sports psychologist for something. I am slowly teaching him how to build routines, such as how to take naps, what to eat, and what to consume before games. There is no doubt that he will improve in the future, but currently, he seems to be just barely making it. When I talked to him one-on-one, he said that he trembles when the game starts, so he needs to gain more experience in international competitions.

In regards to playing in Champions with a full team, what are you learning?

Alecks: Currently, I recognize that the team is facing unprecedented problems. I believe that if you focus too much on winning, you will actually become unable to win. This is because if you try to exert 100% of your strength, you will become too tense and your playing will deteriorate. Therefore, I teach the players to only use 80% of their strength. It means "let's exert the necessary and sufficient strength to win."
