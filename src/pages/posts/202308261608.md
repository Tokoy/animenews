<p>7月30日より順次デビューしたホロライブEnglish（EN）の3期生「Advent」の5名。 （ENでは〇期生というくくりはないので、以下「Advent」のみ表記）</p>
<p>5名はそれぞれの理由で己を閉じ込めていた「監獄」から解き放たれたという存在。 まさに「Advent」（出現する・到来する）のテーマにふさわしいメンバーです。 </p>
<p>いきなり「俺の嫁」を発表して視聴者の度肝を抜いた<span style="color: #c799ff;"><b>シオリ・ノヴェラ</b></span>さん。 </p>
<p>モアイの姿で登場し、魔法少女のような変身アニメーションを経てその姿を現した<span style="color: #52dcff;"><b>古石ビジュー</b></span>さん。 </p>
<p>高い歌唱力で視聴者を魅了した<span style="color: #0076f5;"><b>ネリッサ・レイヴンクロフト</b></span>さん。 </p>
<p>流ちょうな日本語で日本人視聴者のハートを射抜いた<span style="color: #ffb8b8;"><b>フワワ&amp;モココのアビスガード姉妹</b></span>。 </p>
<p>皆さんデビュー配信からファンをザワつかせるタレント性あるメンバーばかりでした。</p>
At this point, it has been almost a month since the debut of the new members. Going forward, they will interact more with other EN members and domestic members (JP members), and unexpected character traits and interesting relationships are expected to be developed. Therefore, in this article, we would like to introduce recommended viewing methods and past memorable scenes mainly for those who say, "I was busy with JP members until now, but I want to know more about EN members!"

The biggest hurdle for EN members' streams is, of course, the language barrier. In such cases, collaborating with JP members can lower the threshold. With interpretation or support from JP members, the "understandable parts" increase significantly. In addition, you can enjoy the relationship between them, so it is recommended.

One of the most enjoyable collaborations with JP members was the numerous large-scale and famous projects. For example, in the 2023 version of popular project "Oozora Keisatsu" by Subaru Oozora, <span style="color: #7300ff;"><b>Inanis Ninomae</b></span> and <span style="color: #007bff;"><b>Gawr Gura</b></span> from EN members appeared.

作为翻译，EN成员的Hakos Baelz也参加了，为Subaru和Hakos的警察搭档表演了一些有趣的对话，试图逃避责任。接下来是从2021年开始，今年已经是第三次的"Holo Server Summer Festival"。每个成员都会以个人或合作形式参加，而在2022年的夏季节日中，Inanis和Sumire Kaga一起享受了"夏日约会"。该计划是一个以Minecraft为舞台的大型计划，由Shishiro Botan提出。除了烟火的发射时间已经确定之外，参与与否、参与时间以及与谁一起玩都是"悠闲系"的计划，摊位也是各个才能自由制作的。在其他EN成员也参加的情况下，Inanis选择了Kaga作为她的搭档，一起享受了赛马比赛、障碍赛、鬼屋等活动。特别是在鬼屋里，她展示了Hololive成员的惊险表演。
<p>在以故事情节为基础的鬼屋中，原本是通过丢弃诅咒人偶来遭受怨灵袭击的情节。然而，为了营造氛围，在垃圾堆放置的标志牌“垃圾清理日为周二和周四”引起了意外的波澜。</p><p>夏祭的举办日是周一。也就是说，“今天是星期几来着？”“今天不是垃圾清理日，所以不扔”等意外行为的Hololive成员不断出现。伊那尓栖和角巻わため也是其中之一。</p><p>对此，担任怨灵“梅丽小姐”的星街彗星改变了以“抛弃人偶的怨恨”为基础的袭击方式，转而采用了“因感激而缠绕”的骚扰路线。似乎是通过即兴处理来应对的。</p>

It is said that watching Pekora's streams has helped cheer up many people when they were feeling down. When visiting the JP server for the first time, other JP members invited her to join them, but she declined, wanting Pekora to be her guide for the "first time". 

A miracle happened amidst all of this. At the time, Pekora was in a love triangle with ID member Moona Hoshinova, who was also very close to Kiara, who was sending passionate love calls. Moona was working on the JP server when she unexpectedly ran into Pekora and Kiara! The viewers were thrilled to witness the unexpected "wife war" that ensued. Moona also joined in on the fun, standing in front of Kiara with a weapon in hand, and making the stream even more exciting for the viewers.

Kiara, who joined Hololive EN because of her admiration for Pekora, is now in a position where she is admired by others.
