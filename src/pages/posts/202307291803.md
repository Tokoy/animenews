2024年，梅雨季节终于结束，迎来了正式的夏天。然而，已经有一些高温天气超过了体温，今年的夏天也将是炎热的。在本文中，我们将介绍一些能给人带来“活力”的性感丰满的人物模型，让你在这个炎热的夏天不输给高温。

<h2 id="">《加瀬大輝插画『蹲着的JK小姐』1/4比例涂装完成品人物模型》</h2>
![加瀬大輝插画『蹲着的JK小姐』1/4比例涂装完成品人物模型](https://animeanime.jp/imgs/zoom/601871.jpg)

这款商品是根据插画家加瀬大輝的作品《蹲着的JK小姐》而制作的人物模型，通过1/4比例和1/6比例的放大，让你更好地欣赏到可爱的脸庞和丰满的臀部。

从翻起的裙子中露出的柔嫩肌肤上的“维纳斯的酒窝”，到被细绳包裹着的丰满臀部，都忠实地再现了插画的细节。此外，还注重了插画中看不到的部分，使得这款人物模型可以从任何角度欣赏。

《加瀬大輝插画『蹲着的JK小姐』1/4比例涂装完成品人物模型》的价格为29,480日元（含税），《加瀬大輝插画『蹲着的JK小姐』1/6比例涂装完成品人物模型》的价格为20,680日元（含税）。预定将于8月22日23时59分前在“Bfull”官方网站接受预订，预计将于11月底发售。
<h2 id="">"Vampire Beauty Girl Morrigan Limited Edition Completed Figure"</h2>
<figure class="ctms-editor-image"><img src="https://animeanime.jp/imgs/zoom/601872.jpg" class="inline-article-image" width="640" height="426"></figure>
<p>This product is an item that changes the popular figure "Vampire Beauty Girl Morrigan" into Lilith's coloring. Morrigan is a character from Capcom's fighting game series "Vampire".</p>
<p>The package design is also new, making it a must-have limited item for fans. It is recommended to display it alongside the regular version figures of Morrigan and Lilith, which are sold separately.</p>
<p>The "Vampire Beauty Girl Morrigan 1/7 Scale Completed Figure" is currently on sale for 15,400 yen (tax included).</p>

<h2 id="">"Kantoku's Original Illustration 'Shizuku-chan' Underwear Ver. 1/7 Scale [Reissue] Painted Completed Figure"</h2>
<figure class="ctms-editor-image"><img src="https://animeanime.jp/imgs/zoom/601912.jpg" class="inline-article-image" width="640" height="960"></figure>
<p>This product is a reissue of the figure of Kantoku's mascot character "Shizuku-chan". Based on an original illustration that captures the moment after she returns home soaking wet, the figure faithfully reproduces the transparency of the wet shirt and underwear, as well as the tears in Shizuku-chan's eyes as she half-cries.</p>
<p>In addition, you can enjoy Shizuku-chan in the middle of changing clothes from every angle, including the skirt half-removed, her feet, and her back peeking through the shirt that has fallen off her shoulder, which are not depicted in the illustration.</p>
<p>The price of the "Kantoku's Original Illustration 'Shizuku-chan' Underwear Ver. 1/7 Scale [Reissue] Painted Completed Figure" is 17,600 yen (tax included). Pre-orders are accepted on the "Bfull" official website until 23:59 on August 8th, and it is scheduled to be released at the end of September.</p>

<h2 id="">"Popkun Illustration 'Nurse-san' Bansoukou Ver. 1/4 Scale Painted Completed Figure"</h2>
<figure class="ctms-editor-image"><img src="https://animeanime.jp/imgs/zoom/601924.jpg" class="inline-article-image" width="640" height="960"></figure>
<p>This product is an item that brings to life the illustration of "Nurse-san" drawn by illustrator Popkun. It is a figure that has been scaled up to 1/4 scale to allow for a fuller view of the ample bosom that can be seen through the white coat, as well as the groin and thighs peeking through the slit.</p>
