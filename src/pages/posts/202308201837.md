The essence of adventure games, which is deep immersion in the story, was condensed in this game. As mentioned earlier, even though adventure games are all classified under the same genre, the scenarios, characters, and game designs are all different. Therefore, it is recommended to always play and collect them with a fresh mindset, as you can have completely different experiences even if you choose titles for achievements. ADV is a major genre that is also available on subscription services. If you subscribe, you can get game achievements at a fixed price, making it a recommended point to enjoy playing and collecting them simultaneously.

Another example that is easy to acquire is completing the game offline. Although multiplayer is now commonplace, online achievements can become difficult to unlock due to reasons such as a decrease in the number of players or service suspension. In some cases, multiplayer may require hundreds or thousands of player kills or victories, so it is important to consider whether all achievements can be obtained offline. In this sense, the "Call of Duty" series is a good example. If split-screen play is possible, connecting two controllers can be a forced solution.
