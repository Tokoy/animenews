<p>Ark System Works has announced that they will release the fighting game "<b>UNDER NIGHT IN-BIRTH II Sys:Celes</b>" for PS4/PS5/Nintendo Switch/Steam in early 2024.</p>
<h2>◆The latest installment of the "UNI" series, depicting the final chapter of the "Hollow Night" story</h2>
<p>This game is the latest installment in the "UNI" series, brought to you by Ark System Works and French Bread. The visual has been updated, and it will depict the final chapter of the "Hollow Night" story that has been continuing for over 10 years.</p>
<p>The game features full voice acting by popular voice actors. With dedicated backgrounds, BGM, and a total playtime of over 10 hours, it can also be enjoyed as a novel-like reading experience.</p>

<p><b>■Synopsis of the story</b><br>The protagonist of the main story, a high school boy named Hyde, meets a mysterious girl named Linne and awakens the power of "Existence". After a fierce struggle, he emerges victorious against the strongest ability user known as "Dazzling Darkness".</p>
<p>However, he suffers a crushing defeat against the immense power of the Revenant, Kuon, who represents "Eternity".</p>
为了实现自己的野望，Quon高声宣布了世界的毁灭。听到这个消息后，Linne消失了。Hyde决心独自踏上战斗之路，为了拯救这个世界的危机......

◆历代角色全员参战！新增进化的图形和操作，支持回滚网络代码

可玩角色包括历代系列中的21个角色全部参战。除了新增技能之外，还进行了系列整体的精细调整，实现了顶级对战平衡。此外，还有新的"伪诞者＜Inverse＞"加入。详细信息将在稍后公布。

此外，本作使用了约600张动画图像来表现每个角色的动作，从表情、发型、飘动的服装到阴影等细节都呈现出流畅的动作。
